## Role at ANOR Technologies

Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

## Biography

Kevin is an entrepreneur and deep-tech founder with over 20 years of experience spanning information and communications technology (ICT), artificial intelligence, and advanced materials. He founded APAC's first SaaS platform for spectroscopy analysis and has led the development of innovative technologies across multiple industries.

At ANOR Technology, Kevin drives the company's vision and growth strategy, focusing on AI-powered solutions at the intersection of regulatory technology (RegTech), intelligent detection systems, and emerging opportunities across Southeast Asia.
